git clone no seu ambiente ROS:
	https://github.com/RAFALAMAO/hector_quadrotor_noetic.git

copie e cole basic.launch no dir: 
	hector_quadrotor_noetic/hector_gazebo/hector_gazebo_worlds/launch
	
copie e cole basic.world no dir
	hector_quadrotor_noetic/hector_gazebo/hector_gazebo_worlds/worlds
	
copie e cole a pasta rviz_cfg no dir:
	hector_quadrotor_noetic/hector_quadrotor/hector_quadrotor_gazebo

copie e cole drone_main.launch no dir
	hector_quadrotor_noetic/hector_quadrotor/hector_quadrotor_gazebo/launch

Run:
catkin_make
source devel/setup.bash
roslaunch hector_quadrotor_gazebo drone_main.launch
rosrun flymode fly.py
